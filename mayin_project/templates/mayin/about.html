Our Story

Mayin Jewellery was founded in 2004 by Balaji Achari, a second-generation goldsmith from a traditional jewellery-making family in Karnataka. What started as a small workshop in Bengaluru has grown into one of Karnataka's most trusted names for handcrafted gold and silver jewellery.

The name Mayin comes from the Sanskrit word for "magical" — reflecting our belief that each piece of jewellery carries its own story, its own energy. We don't just make jewellery; we craft heirlooms that families pass down through generations.

Every piece we create goes through the same hands — Balaji's — ensuring that the quality and soul of the craft is never compromised by mass production.
